

                ~~ Welcome to Dr. Me ~~

        The objective of this interactive pathogen fighting
        game is to defend the body of harmful disease. You'll use
        your knowledge of the innate immune system to correctly
        select which cells to send into combat.

        Good luck, Doctor!

                ~~ Useful Commands ~~

        Cells - displays detailed information on "weapons" you have at your disposal

        Hint - gives you a hint on what to do next or how best to combat the pathogen

        Get <item> - gets the item from your inventory to see if its useful

        Inventory - displays the player's inventory

        Quit - Allows player to quit game
